Our Dishes

Plats Principales:

Couscous Royale:

Indulge in the regal flavors of our Couscous Royale,
a signature Tunisian dish that's a feast for the senses.
Immerse yourself in the aromatic blend of steamed semolina,
tender meats, and a melody of market-fresh vegetables. It's
not just a meal; it's a celebration of Tunisian culinary heritage.

Spicy Seafood Ojja:

Bold and Vibrant Elegance: Embark on a culinary journey with
our Spicy Seafood Ojja. This Tunisian stew is a masterpiece
of rich tomatoes, fiery peppers, and an ensemble of spices.
Succulent seafood, bathed in this flavorful symphony, creates
a dish that's as bold as it is elegant..

Brik Bliss:

Golden Delight Unveiled: Savor the exquisite joy of our Brik à l'Oeuf
a golden symphony of delicate pastry filled with a harmonious mix of
egg, tuna, capers, and parsley. Each bite is a culinary crescendo, a
dance of flavors that will transport you to the sun-kissed shores of
Tunisia..

Desserts:

Makroudh Majesty:

Sweet Oasis of Delight: Treat your palate to the royal sweetness of Makroudh.
Our chefs craft these delectable pastries with love, filling them with a rich
blend of dates, nuts, and spices. Drizzled with honey, each Makroudh is a sweet
oasis in every bite.

Bambalouni Bliss:

Joyful Indulgence: Dive into the joyous world of Bambalouni – our heavenly deep-fried
doughnuts. These fluffy delights, adorned with sugar, are a celebration of sweetness
and simplicity. Indulge in a blissful moment with every bite.

Assidat Zgougou Eleganc

A Symphony of Tradition: Elevate your dessert experience with Assidat Zgougou. This
traditional Tunisian delicacy, blending pine nuts, sesame seeds, and barley grains,
is an elegant symphony of flavors. An exquisite finale to your dining experience..

Starters :

Lablabi Serenity:

Soul-Warming Elixir: Begin your culinary journey with Lablabi, a soul-warming Tunisian
chickpea soup. Infused with cumin, garlic, and olive oil, it's more than a starter;
it's a comforting elixir that sets the tone for an unforgettable meal.

Salade Tunisienne Fresco :

Freshness Unleashed: Our Salade Tunisienne is a vibrant canvas of freshness – tomatoes,
cucumbers, green peppers, olives, and onions, harmoniously seasoned. It's not just a
salad; it's a burst of freshness that awakens your taste buds.

Mloukhia Majesty:

Nutrition in Every Bite: Mloukhia, our green leafy vegetable stew, is a nutritional
powerhouse. Rich in flavors from garlic, coriander, and select spices, it's a regal
start to your Tunisian dining experience. Indulge in the majesty of Mloukhia..
